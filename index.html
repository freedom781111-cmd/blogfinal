About

Silvio Campos

Photographer and writer based in Miami.

Silvio Campos works between sports, documentary and editorial photography. His work is interested in bodies, power and everything left outside the official version.

He photographs because looking is not always enough. He writes because some images also need to be questioned.

When a photograph has teeth, it does not need to write them underneath.
